OUR MISSION

We need to reduce such high incidence of deaths and improve and strengthen our health systems.

Good health and well being among the women fraternity where there is increasing maternal mortality rate and lack of childcare systems leading to death of mother or the child and sometimes both. This solution will aim to involve IT in order to detect and address problems faced by the patients once they conceive and continues to keep a monitoring eye on the patient until she delivers a healthy baby. Moreover the system also tracks down the baby’s and the mother’s health conditions after birth and upto 6 months so that any symptoms that might have severe consequences can be dealt with and both of them are safe. In addition to this, Goal 8: Decent Work and Economic Growth Goal 9: Industry Innovation & Infrastructure, Goal 12: Responsible Consumption and Production, Goal 1: No Poverty would also be influenced to a great extent.
